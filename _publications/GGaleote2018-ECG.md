Design and implementation of a portable analog electrocardiograph (ECG) controlled by Arduino for monitoring cardiac parameters. This work covers the full development cycle from conceptualization, electronic circuit design with an AD8231 analog front end, PCB fabrication, firmware development, and signal processing algorithm implementation.
