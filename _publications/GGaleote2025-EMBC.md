This work presents a signal quality assessment method designed for evaluating QRS detectability in single-lead ECG signals processed on-device. The approach targets ambulatory monitoring applications where reliable QRS detection is critical for accurate cardiac rhythm analysis. The method is optimized for low-power embedded implementations suitable for wearable and portable ECG devices.
